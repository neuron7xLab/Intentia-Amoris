# Privacy and Consent

Intentia Amoris handles intimate data.

## Structural rules

- Partner feelings are not inferred as fact.
- Media requires explicit consent.
- Private data has privacy scope.
- Hormones require lab data.
- Advice must include uncertainty.
- No escalation through pressure.

## Recommended deployment

- self-hosted server;
- encrypted disk;
- private VPN;
- no public bucket;
- regular encrypted backups;
- strict Telegram allowlist;
- separate consent logs.
